History of Altard

The company was first established on March 3, 2003, in Surakarta. As well as being the first Hybrid-based car company in Indonesia. Altard Corporation Indonesia was founded by Athama PD & Aldry AL as co founder and 100% shareholder. It is a challenge for us to create a car that suits our needs with various features that support all activities, is efficient, environmentally friendly, and has the best specifications in all classes. Various innovations and new features are deliberately created and implemented in the form of a car that we call perfect.

It's been 19 years. By 2022, Mazda will produce 1.5 million vehicles for sale worldwide, of which the majority (nearly 1 million) are manufactured in factories located in Surakarta, Jakarta, and Tokyo while the rest are spare parts production in Depok and Bekasi. In 2015, Altard was the 10th largest automaker by volume in the world.
